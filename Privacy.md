# Privacy Policy

**FEAComp** — Last updated: April 2026

## Summary

FEAComp does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data,
usage data, analytics, or crash reports. All computation runs entirely
on your device.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- No personal identifiers (name, email, device ID)
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reports
- No location data
- No contacts, photos, or files outside those you explicitly open
- No advertising identifiers

## Subscription & Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed exclusively by Apple via the App Store.
FEAComp never receives or stores your payment information. Apple's privacy
policy governs all transaction data: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

## Files

FEAComp reads and writes project files (`.femproj`), mesh files (`.stl`),
and solver files (`.inp`, `.bdf`, `.nas`) only when you explicitly open or
save them. These files remain on your device and are never transmitted.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with
a revised date.
